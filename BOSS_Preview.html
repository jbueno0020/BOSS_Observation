Complete Workflow

How to Conduct an Observation
  1. Open the BOSS_Observation_Tool.html file in your web browser
  2. Enter student information and observation details in the setup panel
  3. Position yourself where you can observe both the target student and comparison peer
  4. Click "Start Observation" when ready
  5. When the alert sounds and flashes (every 15 seconds), record what you see at that exact moment
  6. Use the large behavior buttons or keyboard shortcuts to record
  7. Add notes in the notes panel as needed
  8. When finished, click "Stop Observation"
  9. Review the data table and chart
  10. Save the session for your records
  11. Print the report for meetings or documentation
Advanced Features
Session Management
  • Save Sessions: Save observations with custom names
  • Load Sessions: Resume or review previous observations
  • Auto-Backup: Automatic recovery if browser closes unexpectedly
  • Multiple Sessions: Track student progress over time
Edit Observations

Made a mistake? No problem! Click "Edit" next to any observation to change it. Perfect for when you accidentally click the wrong button during fast-paced observations.

Pause & Resume

If an interruption occurs (fire drill, unexpected visitor, etc.), pause the observation and resume when ready. The timer and interval count will pick up exactly where you left off.

Dark Mode / Light Mode

Switch between dark and light themes based on your preference and lighting conditions. The chart background always remains white for optimal printing.

Important: This tool implements momentary time sampling methodology. Users should be trained in BOSS observation procedures and understand when this method is appropriate for their assessment needs.
